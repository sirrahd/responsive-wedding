The Bride and Groom

MEGUMI

Megumi was born on July 7, 1978 in Miki City. Megumi grew up in Osaka. She was a shy child, but made lots of friends in middle and high school and enjoyed challenges. From her first time living alone at Kinki University, she loved travel.

Megumi became a Gastroenterologist at St. Luke's International Hospital and later Yodogawa Christian Hospital. Later, she started graduate school at Kobe University.

As part of her graduate school program, Megumi attended Harvard University Medical School for one year in Boston, USA. That is where she met Drew.

DREW

Drew was born on October 11, 1986 in Raleigh, North Carolina, USA. In his early childhood his family moved to Syracuse, New York, then finally settled in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where Drew attended High School.

Drew attended Pennsylvania State University and majored in Physics. During college he wanted to learn another language, and he chose Japanese because he found the writing system interesting and enjoyed sushi, and then started learning Aikido.

After college, Drew started working as a Program Manager at Microsoft in Cambridge, MA. He started to travel more, going to places like Bulgaria, Peru, Chile, and Germany. Some of his other hobbies include computer programming and systems administration, reading, hiking, and running.

HOW WE MET

Drew had just come back from a trip to Germany and was responsible for hosting a Meetup event in Boston for a free jazz orchestra concert at MIT. A girl from Japan came to the concert late and sat next to Drew. After the event, Drew walked Megumi to her bike since it was dark and she wasn't familiar with the area. Drew invited her to an Okonomiyaki dinner at a local restaurant the next week.

Megumi and Drew spent more and more time together, and by the Spring they finally expressed their feelings for each other. Their relationship quickly grew and they decided to commit their lives to each other. Shortly after, a little ball started growing inside Megumi.

LIVES TOGETHER

Drew and Megumi have enjoyed a lot of adventures together, from exploring New England, the Pacific Northwest, and Grand Circle in the US to Kansai, Kyushu, and Okinawa in Japan. On February 4, their first son, Yuwa, was born.

Drew and Megumi have been living together at Megumi's family home in Japan, and they will soon return to the US where Drew will continue his work at Microsoft.

YUWA

Yuwa was born on February 4, 2014. He's now three months old, and lots of fun.
